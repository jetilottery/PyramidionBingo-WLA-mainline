CASH PYRAMID

Behold the power and mystery of ancient Egypt in Cash Pyramid! The glittering pyramidion draws 70 numbers between 1 and 100. Win a prize when a color block is completed. Shuffle the numbers and prizes to try your luck!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

The object of Cash Pyramid is to mark off all the numbers required to complete a winning color block.

The playing board contains numbers from 1 to 100. To play, draw 70 numbers to mark on the board. Mark off any winning color block to win the corresponding prize. All completed color block prizes are added to total winning prize values.

Play ends when all drawn numbers have been indicated on the board.

Dismiss the win message and view the game’s outcome by pressing the message.

Draw

Press DRAW to draw numbers.

Prizes

Press PRIZES to view the color blocks’ prizes.

Continue

Press CONTINUE to view the numbers and continue the draw.

Shuffle

Press SHUFFLE to shuffle the numbers and prizes.

Speed

Press SPEED to change the draw speed. There are 4 speed options.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Wins are shown in currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
